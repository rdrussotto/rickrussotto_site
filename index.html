About

Hello! I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist studying climate and tropical meteorology in the Ocean and Climate Physics division at Columbia University's Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory. I am currently working with Adam Sobel and Suzana Camargo on characterizing the climatology of tropical cyclones in the NASA GISS ModelE3 global climate model. I have also worked with LDEO's Michela Biasutti on studying the polar amplification of global warming and the influence of continents on climate in the TRACMIP project.
